About the job
Who We Are

Tonal is the world’s first all-in-one home gym with a simply stunning design. It has completely revolutionized the fitness journey, with adaptive weight and coaching cues powered by advanced A.I. technology. We’ve united a diverse team of experts and decades of research to reinvent strength training, making it more efficient, more effective and more engaging. With this in mind, we want to bring that same innovative approach to the workplace. At Tonal, we continue our shift of emphasis by growing our instrumental team. We collectively weave our knowledge and creativity, as we redefine the future of fitness, and Power Progress for our members.

Overview

Tonal is looking for a 2026 Summer Intern who is currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s program at a 4-year university and is interested in Software Engineering. You’ll be working in a fast-paced environment within the software team at Tonal. With the upcoming developments on our product roadmap. You’ll assist key stakeholders and provide support in these areas. You’ll bring a fresh perspective of academic knowledge and familiarity with emerging trends, helping to find innovative solutions while challenging existing methodologies.

This is a 12-16 week internship position based in Toronto, Ontario.

What You’ll Do

Contribute to the execution of Tonal’s FY26 product roadmap through engineering and quality initiatives
Explore, prototype, and build automation solutions using modern AI-assisted development tools to improve test coverage and developer productivity
Collaborate with engineers and product teams to understand features and identify opportunities for automation
Leverage AI to accelerate test creation, validation strategies, and issue triaging across the software development lifecycle
Participate in team meetings, sprint ceremonies, and weekly brainstorming sessions
Prepare reports or presentations and make recommendations to key stakeholders
Communicate and collaborate with team members on a daily basis

Who You Are

Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s degree (rising junior or senior), major in Engineering or related field
Comfortable writing code in at least one programming language
Highly resourceful and proactive in using AI tools (e.g., Codex, Claude Code, or similar) to learn, build, and tackle engineering challenges
Strong problem-solving ability to break down complex problems and iterate quickly
Strong written and verbal communication skills, including email, slack, and presentations
Driven to learn new skills and be adaptable in a corporate environment
Passionate about health, fitness, or consumer hardware
Thrives in a fast paced environment
